About STRIDE

STRIDE (Social Technology and Research for Inclusive Design Excellence) is an initiative aimed at developing innovative, low-tech assistive devices to improve the lives of persons with disabilities (PwDs).

Our mission is to create practical, inclusive solutions that address real-world challenges faced by PwDs, encouraging inclusive innovation and developing functional, low-cost assistive devices.

Focus Areas

Eating

Adaptive utensils, feeding aids and solutions to make eating more accessible.

Communication

Augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) tools for better expression.

Toiletry

Accessible hygiene solutions for independence and dignity.

Learning

Educational tools designed for diverse learning needs and abilities.

Mobility

Low-cost mobility aids to enhance freedom of movement.

Sensory Support

Devices for visual, auditory, and tactile assistance.

Goals of the Designathon

From Ideation to Implementation

  • Empower participants to identify real-world accessibility challenges
  • Utilize design thinking to develop practical solutions
  • Build and prototype assistive devices that can be implemented in institutions supporting PwDs
  • Create sustainable and scalable solutions
  • Foster collaboration between designers, engineers, and PwDs

Our Impact

Encouraging Inclusive Innovation

Bringing together diverse perspectives to create truly inclusive solutions.

Developing Functional, Low-cost Devices

Making assistive technology affordable and accessible to all.

Supporting BUDS Institutions

Providing solutions to BUDS institutions and private schools to benefit PwDs.
